### Summary
I decode problems and provide solutions, make complex things clearer and easier to absorb, and know how to reach an international audience. I can do this for both small and big corporate structures, though am especially good with organisations that are growing quickly and need help keeping all the balls in the air for the most homogenous and efficient growth. 
<br><br>
I do this through a mix of experience, intuition, best practices and strategic guidance, and am very Agile and user-centric minded.

Over the years, I have worked with hundreds of systems, tools, platforms, and programs and pick up new technology quickly. I'm also multilingual and very comfortable with AI.
